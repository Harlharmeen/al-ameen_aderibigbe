The Tale of Burn Carbon, Diamond?

#Independentfieldmapping #AGY23 #FUTAstudent Author: Al-ameen Aderibigbe

The limbs struggle in the crib to grip the steep
As the lad struggles to climb the unearthed heap

The sun smiles at Earth's inhabitants with delight
His melanin shield burns with plight

Under his clothing are gushing springs of sweat
His eyes conserve fluid, a blink will tell about the heat

Dreams and aspirations motivate the climbing
Tiredness dares not approach but stays in hiding

Today, the hunt is for rock
Tomorrow, rewards will hunt him as he relentlessly

rocks

The whole hunt is a strenuous yawn
May God wear his commitment a crown
